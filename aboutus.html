About Salam Al-Ghazali

Welcome to Salam Al-Ghazali, your all-in-one solution for seamless event planning and management at UITM Samarahan Campus 2. Our app is designed to foster a sense of belonging and make it easier than ever to stay connected to your mosque and its activities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform that allows the UITM Samarahan Campus 2 community to access up-to-date information on upcoming events, book events, and receive important announcements. We aim to enhance the sense of community and facilitate smooth and efficient event management.

Features

  • Access up-to-date information on upcoming events
  • Book events easily
  • Receive important announcements and notifications
